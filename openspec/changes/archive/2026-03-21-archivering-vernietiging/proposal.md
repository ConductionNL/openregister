# Proposal: archivering-vernietiging

## Summary

Implement archiving and destruction lifecycle management for register objects, conforming to Archiefwet 1995, MDTO, and NEN-ISO 16175-1:2020.

## Motivation

This feature was identified during the OpenSpec enrichment process as a capability needed for Dutch government compliance and tender requirements.

## Status

Proposed -- not yet implemented. Full spec available in `specs/archivering-vernietiging/spec.md`.
